Our Mission

We know quitting isn’t easy. The cravings, the pressure, the doubts — they’re real. But so is hope.

Next-Breath exists to remind you that your next breath can be different. We’re here to guide, support, and celebrate every step you take toward freedom from addiction. Every choice matters. Every step counts. Every breath is a new chance.

Together, we’re building a future where teens live clear, confident, and free.

“Your next breath is the beginning of your best life.”
