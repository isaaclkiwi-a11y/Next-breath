About

Next-Breath is a student-led movement helping teens break free from vaping and addiction — not with judgment, but with real support, real talk, and real change.

Mission

Next-Breath empowers young people to overcome addiction by creating a safe, honest, and supportive community. Every breath is a chance to start again.
