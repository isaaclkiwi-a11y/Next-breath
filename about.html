About Us

Next-Breath began with a simple idea: that every teen deserves a fresh start. We’ve seen friends fight through vaping and other addictions, and we wanted a space that was real — no lectures, no judgment.

This is more than an organization. It’s a community built by students, for students. We’re here to listen, to walk with you through the hard parts, and to remind you that you are never alone.

“Every breath is a chance to begin again.”
